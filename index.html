Research and Project Highlights

My research is at the intersection of online learning, adaptive systems, control theory, and machine learning:

  • Stability theory in learning, leveraging the intersections between machine learning and adaptive control

  • Parameter convergence in online learning problems using adaptive algorithms

  • Online learning and control in the presence of time-varying features, nonlinear algorithms

  • Control of adaptive systems in the presence of constraints

  • Guidance, Navigation, and Control (GNC) of aerial vehicles
